SpongeBob

Welcome to my SpongeBob fan page!


SpongeBob has been on Nickelodeon for over 20 years! Some of the characters include Squidward, Sandy, and, of course, Patrick!

Sandy!

Sandy is a character on SpongeBob. She lives in a bubble because she cannot breathe underwater.

Squidward!


Squidward is a squid that lives in Spongebob's world. Every time Patrick and SpongeBob see him, he is always grouchy.

Patrick!

Patrick is SpongeBob's best friend. He is pink, goofy, and a funny starfish.
